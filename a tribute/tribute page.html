Biography

Steven Paul Jobs (February 24, 1955 - October 5, 2011) was an American enterreneur, industrial, designer, business magnate, media proprietor and inevstor. He was the co-founder, chairman and CEO of Aple; the chairman and majority shareholder of Pixar; a member of the Walt DisneyCompany;s board of directors following its acquisition of Pixar; and the founder, chairman and CEO of Next. He is widely recognized as a pioneer of the personal computer revolution of the 1970s and 1980s, along with his early business partner and fellow Apple co-founder Steve Wozniak.
